# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Makers, devs, designers, small-business owners who need a QR code fast: a link, Wi-Fi access, a contact card, an email. They reach for QR Studio on the desktop, do one focused job (type content, style it, export PNG/SVG), and leave. Context is task-driven and short. They value a clean live preview and trustworthy output over hand-holding.

## Product Purpose
QR Studio is a desktop QR-code generator (React + Vite frontend, packaged with Tauri v2). It encodes 5 content types (URL, free text, Wi-Fi, email, vCard), styles the QR (dot/corner shapes, independent colors, linear gradient, center logo), and exports PNG/SVG. 100% client-side via `qr-code-styling`. Success = the user gets a styled, scannable QR exported in under a minute, and the app feels like a polished product, not a web toy.

## Brand Personality
Modern and premium. Refined finish, calm confidence, subtle motion, a "this costs money" feel even though it's free. Three words: precise, crafted, effortless. The QR itself is the hero; the chrome stays quiet and lets the preview shine.

## Anti-references
- **Sketchy QR generator sites**: banner ads, amateur layout, raw unstyled forms. The opposite of this.
- **Neon / crypto aesthetic**: black background with garish neon, cliché "futuristic" glow. Deep blue gradient is brand, not neon.
- **Generic SaaS / AI-slop**: identical card grids, hero-metric template, gradient text, default Bootstrap radii.
- **Stock Material Design**: raw Google/Android look, heavy drop shadows, FABs.

## Strategic Design Principles
- Preview-first: the live QR is the largest, calmest element on screen.
- Quiet chrome, loud output: controls recede, the generated code stands out.
- Premium through restraint and craft, not decoration.
- Brand hues (deep blue → teal) carry identity; neutrals are tinted toward them, never pure black/white.
